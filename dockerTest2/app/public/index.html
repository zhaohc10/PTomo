Why PTomo?

The complete solution for scientists and process engineers.

Effortlessly manage real-time multiphase imaging  ECT system

Spin up and integrate out clusters to hundreds of nodes and beyond with just a few clicks, without IT or DevOps. Easily harness the power of Spark for streaming, machine learning, graph processing, and more.

Accelerate your work with an visualization process

Work interactively while automatically visualizating your progress in WebUI — in R, Python, Scala, or SQL. Visualize data in just a few clicks, and use familiar tools like matplotlib, ggplot or d3.

Run your industrial measuring system at scale

Put new applications in production with just 3 steps that: configures the instrument, analyses raw voltage measurements and visualize and control process in real time. Monitor the progress of process and set up automated alerts to notify you of changes.

Collaborate interactively

Seamlessly share measurements system, visualization results in the same ecosystem base.

Plug in ECT module with customized system

Build and detect your findings in visualization in a few steps.
Image multiphase flows in the petrochem sector automatically through jobs.

Publish your visualization results on leading journals

Run your favorite BI tools or sophisticated third-party applications on PTomo Platform.
