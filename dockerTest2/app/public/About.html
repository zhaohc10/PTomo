Meet the Team


Yunjie Yang (CEO)

  • Authored 14 peer-reviewed research papers
  • Recipient of IEEE Instrumentation & Measurement Society Graduate Fellowship Award
  • Fully-funded PhD candidate at University of Edinburgh

He is a member of IEEE, IEEE Instrumentation & Measurement Society, International Society for Industrial Process Tomography and China Instrument & Control Society. He also serves as the reviewer of several international journals and conferences, e.g., IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement, Mechatronics, and IEEE International Conference on Imaging Systems & Techniques.His research spans the area of agile imaging techniques, electrical capacitance tomography, electrical impedance tomography, and cyber physical system for smart buildings..


Huichao Zhao (co-founder)

  • Authored 7 peer-reviewed research papers
  • Veteran Silicon Valley entrepreneur
  • CFD, Machine Learning, Parallel Computation

Huichao Zhao received the B.Eng., M.Sc., and Ph.D. degrees in measurement science and electronics from Changchun Institute of Technology in 2008, Tianjin University in 2010, and Tsinghua University in 2015, Beijing, China, respectively.As a seasoned research professional with strong electronics and computer programming background, he has authored more than 7 research papers including 5 SCI indexed and is the National Scholarship recipient. His current research interests include Electrical Capacitance Tomography, Ultrasonic Flow Measurement, CFD, Machine Learning and Parallel Computation.


Lihui Peng (co-founder)

  • Published more than 50 research papers
  • Recipient of National Scientific and Technological Progress Award
  • Professor in Tsinghua University

He was as an Assistant Lecturer with Tsinghua University in 1990. He has been a member of the three Chinese National Technical Committees since 2001. He has published more than 50 research papers. His biography has been included in Who’s Who in Science and Engineering since 2006. His current research interests include the measurement techniques for multiphase flow, process tomography, multi-sensor data fusion, and flow measurement and instrumentation..
